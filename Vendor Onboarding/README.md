RFP Template:   This RFP template is a simulated governance artifact designed to reflect structured vendor onboarding practices aligned with enterprise risk management principles. It integrates business, financial, operational, security, and regulatory evaluation criteria to support risk-based vendor selection decisions.                                              


Used during:

✔ New vendor selection
✔ Renewal of critical contracts
✔ Replacement of existing vendor
✔ High-risk service procurement
✔ Technology implementation projects

Before contract signing.



Why Is It Used?

✔ Because organizations must:
✔ Ensure vendor capability
✔ Evaluate financial stability
✔ Assess regulatory risk
✔ Identify data protection controls
✔ Avoid concentration risk
✔ Protect against operational failure

It ensures risk-informed decision making before third-party engagement.



Who Initiates It?

Typically initiated by:

✔ Business Unit / Department requesting service
✔ Procurement team
✔ Project sponsor

Then reviewed by:

✔ Risk team
✔ Information security
✔ Legal
✔ Compliance
✔ Finance

Final approval:

✔ Senior management / Risk committee (depending on criticality)
